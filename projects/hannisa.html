HanniSa

Smart School Mobile Application

Project Description

HanniSa is developed in Flutter and uses Google Firebase Database for data storage. This application allows the Registrar to sign up and login, View, Edit and Register Students, Create or Upload Timetable, View, Add and Edit Student Grades, Print Grades Sheet and Receipt. The total marks of each student and the eligibility of the student is automatically calculated when the marks are given as the input. The Registrar is supposed to input the marks for the Assessment (Quiz, Test, Practical Test and Final Exam). The student is allow to signup and login to the application. The student, once logged in, will be able to registrar, view profile; students can access these materials and grades conveniently from any part of the world. Teacher is also allow signing up and login to the application, view timetable, share resources, assignments, and study materials with student digitally, and submit students’ results to the registrar. All these are done conveniently, right on a piece of android smartphone at any time and from anywhere. The Registrar, Teacher/Lecturer and Student may logout of the Application.


Currently, most schools are still using the traditional pen and paper student registration and grades management system. Grades are recorded in the teacher grades textbook, thereafter, recorded by the class sponsor in the grades sheet period after period. Moreover, the registrar will have to enter these same grades in the report card at the end of the academic year, beginning with First Period to Sixth Period and Exam. Imagine a registrar entering the student grades of a school with thousand plus students, more to that, do a manual calculation to generate students’ progress report and yearly financial report. This means can be very tedious, time consuming and somehow lead to errors while generating reports. Apart from this, sometimes the teacher grades book gets lost or destroyed; sponsor misplaced student(s) grades sheet, or information wrongly entered due to various reasons. Therefore, this system aims to eliminate paper work and save time, generate accurate reports automatically based on input without consuming lot of time and reduces the amount of time it takes to access student records.

Key Features:

  • Student's Registration and Grades Entry Form
  • Automated student's receipt and report generation
  • Automated Three Top Average Students of the school every period
  • Automated total students, total famale and total male generation
  • Automated total students per class with a chat display generation
  • Automated Yearly Fee Summary generation

Technologies Used:

Microsoft Office, Excel VBA, Visual Basic
