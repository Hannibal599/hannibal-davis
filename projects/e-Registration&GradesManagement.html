Electronic registration & Grades Management System

Desktop Base Excel School System

Project Description

Electronic Registration and Grades Management System is a user-friendly system that offers students Registration and grades entry. It continuously generate student receipt and report card. It also generates and displays on the dashboard clear summary of total student registered, the sub-division of both Male and Female students that have registered, a chart of student statistics that summary the total number of student registered per class and the yearly fee summary. This systm aims at replacing the Traditional Pen Pouches and Textbooks Registration and Grades Management System for a new technology of Electronic Registration Grades Management System.

Key Features:

  • Student's Registration and Grades Entry Form
  • Automated student's receipt and report generation
  • Automated Three Top Average Students of the school every period
  • Automated total students, total famale and total male generation
  • Automated total students per class with a chat display generation
  • Automated Yearly Fee Summary generation

Technologies Used:

Microsoft Office, Excel VBA, Visual Basic
